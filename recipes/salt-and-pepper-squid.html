Salt and Pepper Squid

A delicious seafood meal to go with any salad

Have with a side of sweet chilli sauce!

10 min prep, 10 min cook, 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 large (750g) cleaned squid hoods
  • vegtable oil, for shallow frying
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1 1/2 tbsp freshly cracked peppercorn medley
  • 1 tbsp salt flakes
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • Salad leaves, to serve
  • Lemon wedges, to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C/140°C fan-forced.
  2. Cut each squid hood in half lengthways. Using a sharp knife score inside in a criss-cross pattern. Cut each piece in half lengthways. Cut each strip crossways into 4 pieces. Pat dry with paper towel.
  3. Heat oil in a wok or saucepan over medium-high heat.
  4. Combine flour, pepper, salt and chilli in a shallow dish. Lightly toss squid in flour mixture, shaking off excess. Cook, turning, for 2 minutes or until light golden. Transfer to a baking tray. Place in oven to keep warm while cooking remaining squid. Serve with salad greens and lemon wedges.
